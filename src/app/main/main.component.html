About Me

Howdy, my name is Clayton Kristiansen and I am a Senior Computer Engineering major at Texas A&M University. I have a passion for writing code, and have been doing so since almost the day I could type. I find all forms of programming enjoyable, whether it be programming microcontrollers or making web apps. In school I focused primarily on writing low level code and hyper-efficient algorithms. I also took a few classes about Machine Learning and AI, at least enough to be afraid of them. In industry I just want to have fun solving problems by writing code.

I grew up in Duvall, Washington and have always loved spending my time in the outdoors. My time in Boy Scouts helped instill a deep and genuine appreciation for nature and everything in it. Whether it be hiking, backpacking, skiing, snowboarding, surfing, or kayaking you will often find me out in the wilderness. I also have a healthy addiction to strategy games. My all time favorites include Europa Universalis, Hearts of Iron, and of course... Minecraft.
